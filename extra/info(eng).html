Information about the website

This is a website where relationships between the countryside and the city and their development together are discussed.

This website was the project classified and awarded first place in the region of Curitiba in the programming competition Agrinho of 2024, representing the Colégio Estadual Hasdrubal Bellegard, in partnership with FAEP, SENAR, Rural Union and SEED of Paraná

The website is made available by: Github Pages and Vercel.
